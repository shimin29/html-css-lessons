Robert Downey Jr. is an American actor and producer who has become one of the most iconic figures in Hollywood. He is best known globally for his role as Tony Stark / Iron Man in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, a role he played from 2008 to 2019. Robert Downey Jr.’s past is often cited as one of the most significant turnaround stories in Hollywood history. Before he became the face of the MCU, he went through a very public and dark period involving substance abuse and legal trouble.
